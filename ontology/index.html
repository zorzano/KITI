Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

This is the Ontology emplyed in KITI, a Knowledge Graph for the Internet of Things ecosystem. It is based in pre-defined classes and relations.

Introduction back to ToC

Internet of Things projects demand the coordination of many different technology providers, from module manufacturers to cloud computing platforms. Service providers must know hundreds of partners and combine their products according to the customer's needs. To help engineering teams, we have built KITI, a domain knowledge graph for the IoT ecosystem that gathers providers, products, services, and their relationships. The article introduces how we construct the graph from simple, low-quality sources. We demonstrate its business value through different use cases, from simple data search to automatic project design, and gain positive results, as well as through a user survey. This experience shows the value of Knowledge Graph modelling complex industry ecosystems and automatically reasoning on this information.
